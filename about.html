Our History

The Hale Family Has Been Doing This Work Since 1997

Fred Hale Jr. started Hale Bulkheading with the vision of protecting your coastal barriers from the harmful erosion that causes damage to the natural ecosystems and can result in your own property damage.

That straightforward approach built a reputation through word of mouth. Today the company is still family-operated, still using the same methodical process for every project, and still doing work you can point to decades later.

We're licensed and bonded in Virginia, and we handle our own permitting. You don't need an outside consultant — we've been through the regulatory process hundreds of times and know what gets approved.
